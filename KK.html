Pichhwai painting

Pichhwai (also pichhavai, pichhvai, pechhavai etc) are large devotional Hindu painted pictures.

This painting is normally done on cloth, which portray Krishna.

They are mainly made to hang in Hindu temples of the Pushtimarg devotional tradition, especially the Shrinathji Temple in Nathdwara, Rajasthan, built around 1672.
